Conditioning

With any sporting program, it is important to make sure that the body is in a condition to engage in an activity. Below are ideas to help prepare and maintain the body of your athlete.

Stretching Routines

  • Active Stretches

    1. 3/4 Sprint
    2. Walking Knee Hug
    3. Walking Tin Soldier
    4. Forward Lunge
    5. Forward Lunge w/ Overhead Reach
    6. Forward Lunge w/ Trunk Rotation
    7. Backward Skip w/ External Hip Rotation (Open Gate)
    8. Push-ups
    9. Partner Push-ups
    10. Twisting Push-up
    11. High Knee Jog
    12. Butt Kicks
    13. Swimmers
  • Static Stretches

    1. Arms Across
    2. Both Arms Behind
    3. Arms Behind (Tricep Stretch)
    4. Toe Touches
    5. Knee Hugs
    6. Scissors
    7. Hopscotch
    8. Shoulder Shrugs
    9. Air Squats
