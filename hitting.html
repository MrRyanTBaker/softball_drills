  10. *Numbered Balls
    • Purpose

      • Keeping their eye on the ball.


    • Equipment
      • Two participants
      • Bat
      • Balls w/ numbers written on them
      • Batting helmet
      • Screen (Optional)


    • Organization

      • The batter stands in either an imaginary batters box with a plate, or at home. The batter will face either a screen, approximately 10 to 12 feet from the batter, and a tosser alongside them, in a position to toss the ball infront of the plate.


    • Execution

      • A player or coach soft tosses a ball to the batter, who will hit the ball into the screen. The batter must call out the number on the ball before they are allowed to make contact.


    • Variations

      • The tosser can add spin to the ball when they toss it.
      • The tosser can toss multiple balls and call out the number the player must hit.
      • Change the direction that the toss is made from (side, front, behind).
    • Coaching Points
